Triangle Carrot Limited (‘TC’) has created this Privacy Notice in order to demonstrate our commitment to protecting the privacy of all users of GetTableService services (‘our services’).

Part One: Introduction

  • TC will always respect your right to privacy. However, we understand that the internet is not a secure medium and therefore we have implemented security measures to, where possible, protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information placed under our control.
  • All data gathered as a result of using our services will be treated as confidential.
  • All orders placed online will be confirmed by email if you have provided an email address.

Part Two: How we use your data

  • To place an order, you will be required to supply contact and personal information.
  • Your name and email address will be disclosed to the restaurant you place an order with (‘the host’).
  • The details you enter specifically for COVID-19 Track and Trace purpose will be held by TC for 21 days and then destroyed.
  • TC will not use your personal data for any marketing purpose and we will not share your data with anyone other than the host who may require it to get in touch with you if there is a problem relating to your order or relating to potential exposure to Coronavirus onsite.
  • TC may disclose your personal data where it complies with a court order or other legal process or request by law enforcement authorities or to defend itself against any legal claims.
  • NONE OF YOUR PERSONAL CREDIT/DEBIT CARD IS COLLECTED OR STORED BY TC OR PASSED ON TO THE HOST.

Part Three: How do you keep my data safe?

  • The security of all data is very important. TC (and Stripe, the payment service provider) implements the following measures, protection of servers by firewalls, SSL connections and encryption of sensitive data. This list is not exhaustive.
