Why choose

Get Table Service



Order and pay by mobile is not just about reducing contact between your staff and your customers, it can help you…

Save money

Reduce your staff’s workload by offering order and pay on mobile which means you can reduce your labour costs without worrying about keeping customers waiting.

Save time

Free up your staff’s time so they can focus on preparing the orders more efficiently and keeping the place clean which means happier customers and faster turnovers.

Be creative with your menu

Change your offerings on the online menu as and when to help you use up excess inventory and reduce precious food waste

Provide better customer experience

Your waiting staff’s contact time with the customers can instead be used to oversee their experience so that they have a more memorable time. You’ll get more positive reviews as well as an edge over your competitors.

Worry Less

Staff off sick? Trouble hiring good ones? You’re still covered. Worry less and take home a more positive vibe for the family to truly enjoy your downtime.

The Customer Interface

Step 1: Scan QR code

This will take them straight to your menu, either on your website or on GetTableService. Alternatively they can go via the web browser or download the app.

Step 2: View menu and choose items

A straight-forward "shopping" experience adding items to trolley. Comments can be added along with each item.

Step 3: Check out with card payment or arrange to pay cash/contactless in person

Accepting major credit/debit cards (20p + 1.4% commission, more details here).

On PC, Tablet or Phone

Host Interface

1. Watch the orders come in real-time on the host app, no need to refresh your screen.

2. Tap 'accept' on each order just to let your customers know you've got it.

3. All that remains is to prepare and serve!

Other Handy Features

Live Menu Edit

Amend your menu from the host interface for instant update on the customer's side.

Track and Trace (Optional)

We can add an extra step in the checkout process to collect customer contact details for you.

Easy record transfer

Your past orders are saved to your account and can be exported into a spreadsheet. A direct link is also provided with each order so you can easily check the Stripe transactions.
