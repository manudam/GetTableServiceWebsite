The Customer Interface

Step 1: Visit website or download app

Your customers will be able to find your restaurant to access your menu. They skip this step if going via your existing website.

Step 2: View menu and choose items

A straight-forward "shopping" experience, adding items to trolley. Comments can be added along with each item.

Step 3: Check out with Stripe payment

Which accepts major credit/debit cards (20p + 1.4% commission, more details here)
